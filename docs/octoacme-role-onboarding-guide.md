# OctoAcme — Role Onboarding Guide

## Purpose
Provide a structured onboarding process for new team members joining OctoAcme projects, ensuring they understand their role, responsibilities, and how to collaborate effectively with the team.

## When to Use
Use this guide when:
- A new team member joins a project
- An existing team member takes on a new role
- Refreshing understanding of role expectations

---

## General Onboarding Checklist

### First Day
- [ ] Welcome meeting with Project Manager
- [ ] Access to project repository and documentation
- [ ] Introduction to team members and key stakeholders
- [ ] Review of [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Review of [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)

### First Week
- [ ] Complete review of all project documentation in `docs/`
- [ ] Attend team standup and observe team rhythm
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with key collaborators
- [ ] Understand current project status and priorities
- [ ] Review project board and backlog
- [ ] Access to communication channels (Slack, email lists, etc.)

### First Month
- [ ] Shadow experienced team members in key activities
- [ ] Contribute to at least one deliverable
- [ ] Participate in a retrospective
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding experience

---

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Project Sponsor Onboarding
- [ ] Review project charter and business justification
- [ ] Understand budget and resource allocation
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to establish communication cadence
- [ ] Review escalation paths and decision thresholds
- [ ] Understand key milestones and success metrics

### Project Manager Onboarding
- [ ] Review [Project Initiation Guide](octoacme-project-initiation.md)
- [ ] Review [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md)
- [ ] Understand risk register and current risks
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to align on priorities
- [ ] Review team composition and availability

### Product Manager Onboarding
- [ ] Review product roadmap and backlog
- [ ] Understand customer needs and success metrics
- [ ] Meet with stakeholders to understand requirements
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Align with Project Manager on delivery timeline

### Developer Onboarding
- [ ] Set up development environment
- [ ] Review codebase and architecture documentation
- [ ] Understand branching and PR conventions
- [ ] Review Definition of Done and quality standards
- [ ] Pair with experienced developer on first task
- [ ] Complete first code review (both as author and reviewer)

### Quality Assurance Lead Onboarding
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) — Quality & Testing section
- [ ] Review [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)
- [ ] Understand current testing strategy and tools
- [ ] Review quality gates and acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Meet with development leads to align on quality expectations
- [ ] Review past retrospective action items

### Change Control Lead Onboarding
- [ ] Review [Change Control Checklist](octoacme-change-control-checklist.md)
- [ ] Understand approval thresholds and escalation paths
- [ ] Review current change log and pending requests
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to align on change management process
- [ ] Understand project scope boundaries

### Communications Coordinator Onboarding
- [ ] Review [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)
- [ ] Understand stakeholder communication needs
- [ ] Review current communication plan and templates
- [ ] Access to all communication channels
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to align on reporting cadence
